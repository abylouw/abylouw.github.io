Neural Speech Synthesis For Resource-Scarce Languages

The following speech samples relate to a paper submitted for review at the South African Forum for Artificial Intelligence Research 2019

Systems are as follows (as described in the paper),

System name System description
DNN LPCNet Stacked bottleneck feed-forward neural network based voice with LPCNet vocoder
DNN World Stacked bottleneck feed-forward neural network based voice with World vocoder
HMM LPCNet HTS HMM-based voice with LPCNet vocoder
HMM World HTS HMM-based voice with World vocoder
